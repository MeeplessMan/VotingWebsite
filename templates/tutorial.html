Tutorial: How to Vote

Step 1: Login or Sign-up
To start using the voting system, you need to log in or sign up. If you're new, click on "Sign Up" and create an account. If you're an existing user, just enter your credentials and log in.

Step 2: Choose Your Candidate
Once you're logged in, go to the "Vote Now" section and select your preferred candidate. You can see the options available and their details to make an informed decision.

Step 3: Cast Your Vote
After selecting your candidate, click the "Vote" button to cast your vote. You'll receive a confirmation message once your vote is successfully recorded.

Step 4: View Results
Once you've voted, you can view the results in real-time by going to the "View Results" section. The voting process is transparent and secure.

Step 5: Get Help
If you encounter any issues or need assistance, visit the "Help" section for a detailed FAQ or contact support through the provided channels.

If you still have questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our Contact Us page.
